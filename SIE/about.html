OUR STORY

Redefining Moroccan streetwear with effortless style.

Founded in Casablanca in 2026, OVRSZD was born out of a simple frustration: the daily struggle of putting together an outfit that looks stylish, feels incredibly comfortable, and works for any occasion.

We realized that modern Moroccan men were tired of choosing between looking good and feeling comfortable. Fast fashion offered cheap trends that fell apart after a few washes, while luxury brands charged a premium for logos rather than quality.

We set out to create the ultimate all-in-one solution. By engineering the perfect oversized fit and sourcing premium, heavyweight cotton, we created a lineup of streetwear essentials that drape perfectly on any body type.

At OVRSZD, we believe in minimalism, durability, and a clean aesthetic. No loud logos, no complicated designs—just pure, effortless style that gives you instant confidence the moment you put it on.
